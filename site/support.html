Support

Primary support channel: support.cue.app@gmail.com

Secondary support channel: GitHub Issues

What to include

Platform, app version, reproduction steps, and redacted screenshots or diagnostics details.

Billing and refunds

Billing and refund handling are managed by the App Store and Google Play.

Local data deletion

Use Disconnect in the app to remove local Cue authentication state and reminder artifacts. Cue does not operate a backend account service.

Independence

Cue is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Real-Debrid.
